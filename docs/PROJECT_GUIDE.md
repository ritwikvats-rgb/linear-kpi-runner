### In Simple Terms

Think of it as a **smart assistant** that:
1. Connects to your Linear workspace
2. Reads all your projects, issues, and comments
3. Calculates metrics (KPIs) automatically
4. Presents them in a beautiful, easy-to-read format
5. Uses AI to summarize what's happening and give recommendations

---

## Why Was It Built?

### The Problem

Engineering managers face these challenges:
- Opening Linear and clicking through multiple pods/teams is time-consuming
- No single view showing all pods' status at once
- Manually calculating delivery percentages is tedious
- Hard to quickly identify which pods need attention
- Comments and discussions are scattered across many issues

### The Solution

KPI Assistant solves this by:
